The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced the schedule of Tata Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025. The 18th edition of the prestigious tournament will begin on March 22, 2025 and the final will be played on May 25, 2025. The 74 matches of this season will be played across 13 venues and will include 12 double-headers. The afternoon games will start at 03.30 PM IST, while the evening games will start at 07.30 PM IST. The mega event of cricket will begin on March 22, 2025 with defending champions Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) hosting Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) at the iconic Eden Gardens in Kolkata.
